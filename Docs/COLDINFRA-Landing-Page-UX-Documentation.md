## üé® Design Principles & Guidelines

### Visual Hierarchy:
1. **Headlines:** Bold, large fonts that grab attention
2. **Benefits:** Clear, scannable bullet points
3. **CTAs:** Contrasting colors that stand out
4. **Social Proof:** Prominent placement with visual elements

### Color Strategy:
- **Primary:** Professional blue (#1E40AF) - trust, reliability
- **Accent:** Success green (#10B981) - positive action, growth
- **Warning:** Orange (#F59E0B) - urgency, attention
- **Neutral:** Gray scale for supporting text

### Typography:
- **Headlines:** Sans-serif, bold, 32-48px
- **Body:** Sans-serif, regular, 16-18px
- **CTAs:** Sans-serif, medium, 16-18px
- **Captions:** Sans-serif, regular, 14px

### Spacing & Layout:
- **Generous whitespace** for readability
- **Consistent margins** (80px sections, 40px subsections)
- **Grid-based layout** for alignment
- **Mobile-first responsive** design

## üìù Content Guidelines

### Writing Tone:
- **Professional but approachable**
- **Benefit-focused, not feature-focused**
- **Action-oriented with clear next steps**
- **Honest about limitations and challenges**

### Headline Formulas:
- **Value + Time:** "Launch campaigns in 10 minutes"
- **Outcome + Price:** "Maximum deliverability at $2.50/mailbox"
- **Problem + Solution:** "Stop Gmail bans with purpose-built accounts"
- **Social Proof + Benefit:** "Join 14K+ users with higher open rates"

### CTA Best Practices:
- **Action verbs:** Start, Get, Launch, Calculate
- **Benefit-focused:** "Start Your Free Trial" vs "Sign Up"
- **Urgency when appropriate:** "Launch Today" vs "Start Sometime"
- **Risk reduction:** "No credit card required"

### Feature Descriptions:
- **Start with the benefit**
- **Explain the feature**
- **Quantify the impact**
- **Include social proof when possible**

Example:
